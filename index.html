About Puzz

Puzz isn’t just a name — it’s a promise. Leading a team of devoted visual storytellers, Puzz has spent over a decade documenting life’s most profound moments: from the intimacy of a burial to the euphoria of a wedding, from the first birthday candle to the golden anniversary. We don’t just take photos; we curate legacies. Based in Accra, available worldwide.
